Reproduction #2095: Action buttons hidden under overlapping messages

This is a minimal CSS reproduction of the stacking context bug in OpenChamber's chat UI.

The transform: translateY() on the virtualizer wrapper creates a stacking context. Each turn's section.relative creates a child stacking context. Turn N+1's sticky header (z-20 in its context) paints ON TOP of Turn N's assistant block (z-0 in its context) because Turn N+1 comes after Turn N in DOM order within the transform stacking context.

Result: The action buttons at the bottom of Assistant N are hidden behind User N+1's sticky header.
